Correction from Robert: 26-June-2016

I had a short look at the version which is currently online. Here is some short feedback:
  • We usually use the number style for sections (1.2.3.4) - your style (A.1.2.3) is more common in other disciplines (btw: law uses A. / I. / 1. / a) / aa) / (1) / (a) which is quite strange)
  • Looking at your table of contents, It is not clear which parts are your own work. Section C and D should have a better title which reflects that this is your contribution.
  • Update the title: "Design and Implementation" section explains on design architecture, implementation details are not included.(Usually not needed for Thesis)
  • "Evaluation and Results" is a clear title but quite weak. You could write something like "Evaluating our approach on a Spark & HDFS cluster". This tells the reader more about what you have done.
  • Support important concepts with additional sources besides Wiki(say Z-curve with its base paper)
  • Pay attention to the size of the font in your graphics. You should aim for a minimum font size which is about as big as your footnote size.
